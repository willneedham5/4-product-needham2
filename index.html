Resorts

Sugarloaf Mountain Hotel

A full-service resort hotel located right in the heart of the Sugarloaf Village, the Sugarloaf Mountain Hotel evokes the warmth of a country farmhouse and the comfort of home. With a design inspired by the classic New England farmhouses that dot Franklin County, Maine, the Sugarloaf Mountain Hotel utilizes honest, solid materials and fixtures, sourced locally whenever possible. Our recently-renovated hotel restaurant, 45 North, features walls paneled with lumber salvaged from the Penobscot River and live-edge bar tops constructed from locally harvested timber. Our custom-built 30-person outdoor hot tub offers a relaxing conclusion to your round of golf or day on the mountain, while exclusive Boyne Beds provide a luxurious sleeping experience – allowing you to wake up refreshed and ready to tackle the day’s adventures.

Sugarloaf Inn

Convenient, cozy, and affordable describe the slopeside Sugarloaf Inn. Located directly on the Birches Trail, the Sugarloaf Inn is one of Sugarloaf’s original lodges and offers outstanding mountain views and slopeside convenience. Enjoy a hearty meal and camaraderie at the in-house Shipyard BrewHaus Restaurant between runs all winter, while summer amenities include tennis courts.

Condominums

You can select from hundreds of condominiums ranging in unit size from studios to five bedrooms or private homes. Best of all, every property is located right on the resort and is within walking distance of the slopes.
